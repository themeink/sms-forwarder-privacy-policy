Privacy Policy for SMS Forwarder

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how SMS Forwarder ("we," "our," or "the App") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our Android application. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal data.

By using SMS Forwarder, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 SMS Messages

  • What we collect: Content, sender information, timestamp, and SIM card information of received SMS messages
  • Why we collect it: To enable the core functionality of forwarding messages based on your configured rules
  • How we use it: Messages are processed locally on your device to match against your filtering rules and forward to your specified destinations

2.2 Contact Information

  • What we collect: Contact names and phone numbers from your device's address book
  • Why we collect it: To allow you to select specific contacts for rule-based SMS forwarding
  • How we use it: Contacts are accessed only for rule configuration and are not transmitted to external servers

2.3 Phone State and Identity

  • What we collect: Dual SIM card information, roaming status, and device phone state
  • Why we collect it: To detect which SIM card received messages and determine roaming status for rule-based controls
  • How we use it: This information is used locally to execute forwarding rules appropriately

2.4 Email Addresses and Phone Numbers

  • What we collect: Email addresses and phone numbers you provide as forwarding destinations
  • Why we collect it: To forward SMS messages to your specified destinations
  • How we use it: Used exclusively for sending forwarded messages; stored locally on your device

2.5 Forwarding History and Logs

  • What we collect: Timestamps, sender information, message previews, delivery status, and rule identifications
  • Why we collect it: To provide activity logs, statistics, and failed forwarding retry functionality
  • Retention period:
    • Free users: 7 days, maximum 50 logs
    • Premium users: 90 days, unlimited logs
  • How we use it: Stored locally on your device for reference and troubleshooting

2.6 App Usage and Preferences

  • What we collect: Your app settings, rule configurations, theme preferences, and notification settings
  • Why we collect it: To maintain your personalized app experience and preferences
  • How we use it: Stored locally on your device

2.7 Backup Data

  • What we collect: When you use the backup feature, SMS Forwarder creates a local backup file containing your rule configurations and app settings
  • Why we collect it: To allow you to restore your settings and rules on the same device or transfer them to another device
  • How we use it: The backup file is stored locally on your device's storage (typically in the Downloads folder or location you select). Backup files are in JSON format and remain on your device—they are never transmitted to our servers or any third party

2.8 Purchase Verification Data

  • What we collect: When you make an in-app purchase (Premium subscription), we collect purchase tokens provided by Google Play
  • Information transmitted to our server:
    • Purchase token (unique identifier from Google Play)
    • Product ID (subscription type identifier)
    • Package name (app identifier)
  • Why we collect it: To verify your purchase with Google Play's servers and prevent unauthorized access to premium features
  • How we use it: This information is sent to our secure verification server for real-time validation only. We do not store purchase tokens permanently, and no personally identifiable information is collected during this process

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • SMS Forwarding: Processing and forwarding SMS messages based on your configured rules
  • Rule Filtering: Applying filters (source conditions, text patterns, regex, message length, time scheduling) to determine which messages to forward
  • Activity Logging: Maintaining forwarding history with delivery status for your reference
  • Statistics: Providing dashboard metrics (total forwarded, success rate, failed count)
  • App Functionality: Executing dual SIM detection, roaming control, and message template customization
  • Backup & Restore: Creating local backup files for your rule configurations and settings
  • Purchase Validation: Verifying premium subscriptions with Google Play to enable premium features
  • Crash Reporting: Collecting anonymous crash data to identify and fix bugs

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All your data is stored exclusively on your device. We do not store any of your personal information, SMS content, contacts, or forwarding logs on external servers.

Rule configurations, app settings, and logs are stored in your device's local storage using Android's secure storage mechanisms.

4.2 Backup File Security

Backup files created by the app contain your rule configurations and settings. These files are stored in plain text JSON format on your device. We recommend:

  • Storing backup files in a secure location
  • Not sharing backup files with untrusted parties
  • Deleting backup files when no longer needed
  • Being aware that backup files can be read by anyone with access to your device storage

4.3 Network Communications

The app makes network requests only for:

  • Email forwarding: Via Gmail API (when configured by you)
  • Purchase verification: Validating premium subscriptions with our secure server
  • Crash reporting: Sending anonymous crash data to Firebase Crashlytics

All network communications use industry-standard HTTPS encryption.

4.4 Security Measures

  • The app requests only necessary permissions required for its core functionality
  • Data transmission for email forwarding uses secure protocols (TLS/SSL)
  • SMS forwarding uses your device's standard SMS infrastructure
  • Purchase verification uses encrypted HTTPS connections
  • No behavioral analytics or tracking SDKs are integrated into the app

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Google Play Billing

SMS Forwarder uses Google Play's In-App Billing service for premium subscriptions. Google Play may collect payment information and purchase history according to Google's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your payment card details or banking information.

Google Play Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5.2 Purchase Verification Server

We operate a secure verification server to validate in-app purchases with Google Play's servers. This server:

  • Validates purchase tokens with Google Play in real-time
  • Does not store any personally identifiable information
  • Does not access or store SMS content
  • Does not retain purchase tokens permanently
  • Uses HTTPS encryption for all communications
  • Only processes purchase verification requests

5.3 Firebase Crashlytics

We use Firebase Crashlytics to automatically collect crash reports when the app encounters errors. This helps us improve app stability and fix bugs.

Information Collected by Crashlytics:

  • Device information: Device model and manufacturer (e.g., "Samsung Galaxy S23")
  • Operating system: Android version (e.g., "Android 14")
  • App version: Version of SMS Forwarder installed (e.g., "0.0.5")
  • Crash data: Technical stack traces and error logs
  • Timestamp: Date and time when crashes occurred

What Crashlytics DOES NOT Collect:

  • ❌ No user IDs or account identifiers
  • ❌ No personally identifiable information
  • ❌ No SMS message content
  • ❌ No contact information
  • ❌ No email addresses or phone numbers
  • ❌ No usage analytics or behavior tracking
  • ❌ No screen navigation data
Privacy Note: Crash data is completely anonymous and cannot be linked to you as an individual. This data collection is necessary for app functionality and falls under legitimate interest under GDPR. No user consent is required for anonymous crash reporting.

Data Retention: Crash data is automatically deleted after 90 days from Firebase servers.

Firebase Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

5.4 Gmail API (Optional)

If you configure email forwarding, the app uses Gmail API to send emails on your behalf. Your Gmail credentials and email data are handled according to Google's privacy policy. We do not store your Gmail credentials or access your emails beyond the forwarding functionality you explicitly configure.

5.5 No Analytics or Advertising

  • We do not integrate any third-party analytics services (other than Crashlytics for crash reporting)
  • We do not display advertisements
  • We do not track your usage, behavior, or app navigation

SMS Forwarder does not share, sell, or transmit your personal information to any third parties, with the following exceptions:

6.2 User-Initiated Forwarding
